Frequently Asked Questions About Sea Freight

How long does sea freight delivery take?

Sea freight delivery times depend on the route and destination, but typically range from 15 to 45 days. For example, shipping from Asia to North America often takes around 30 days.

Is my cargo insured during sea freight?

Yes, we provide comprehensive marine cargo insurance to protect your goods against risks like damage, loss, or theft during transit.
