Smart Excess Baggage Solutions

Avoid expensive airline excess baggage fees with our professional shipping services. Whether you're relocating, traveling for extended periods, or need to send personal items internationally, we offer secure and cost-effective alternatives to airline excess baggage charges.

Why Ship Your Excess Baggage?

Airlines can charge £15-50 per kilogram for excess baggage, making it expensive to travel with extra items. Our shipping services often cost 50-70% less than airline excess baggage fees, while providing better protection and tracking for your belongings.

What We Can Ship:

  • Personal Clothing & Accessories
  • Books & Documents
  • Sports Equipment
  • Electronics (with restrictions)
  • Gifts & Souvenirs
  • Professional Equipment

Service Options

Choose from economy sea freight for non-urgent items (15-35 days), or express air freight for faster delivery (3-7 days). We also offer consolidated services where your baggage travels with other shipments to reduce costs further.

Frequently Asked Questions

What items cannot be shipped as excess baggage?

We cannot ship dangerous goods, liquids, aerosols, batteries, perishable items, or restricted materials. This includes perfumes, alcohol, lithium batteries, and pressurized containers. We'll provide a full list of prohibited items when you book.

How much can I save compared to airline excess baggage fees?

Savings typically range from 50-70% compared to airline excess baggage rates. For example, shipping 20kg to Europe might cost £40-60 with us versus £150-300 with airlines. We provide free quotes so you can compare costs.

Do you provide packing materials and services?

Yes, we offer complete packing services including sturdy boxes, protective materials, and professional packing. This ensures your items are properly protected during transit and helps prevent damage claims.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times depend on the service chosen: Economy sea freight takes 15-35 days, standard air freight takes 5-10 days, and express air freight takes 3-7 days. We also offer same-day collection in many UK areas.

Is my baggage insured during shipping?

Yes, all shipments include basic insurance coverage. We also offer additional insurance for high-value items. Our packaging standards and careful handling minimize the risk of damage during transit.
