Frequently Asked Questions

What are your collection hours?

Standard collection hours are 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. We also offer extended hours and weekend collections for urgent shipments at additional cost. Same-day collection is available for bookings made before 2:00 PM.

Do you provide packaging materials?

Yes, we offer a complete packaging service including boxes, bubble wrap, and protective materials. Our drivers can also pack your items on-site if needed. We use high-quality materials to ensure your goods arrive safely.

How do I track my collection and delivery?

Once your collection is booked, you'll receive a unique tracking number via SMS and email. You can track your shipment in real-time through our website or mobile app, with automatic updates at each stage of the journey.

What if I'm not available for collection?

We offer flexible solutions including safe place deliveries, neighbor collection, and collection from reception areas. You can also arrange for someone else to hand over your packages using our authorized person system.
